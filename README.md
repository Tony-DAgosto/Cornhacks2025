### 2. ğŸ° Bananas vs. Monkeys

A strategic tower defense game where you protect your territory from waves of invading monkeys!

**How to Play:**

- Place different banana towers on the grid to defend against monkey waves
- Earn banana chips by defeating monkeys or collecting coins
- Survive all 10 waves to win!

**Banana Tower Types:**
| Tower | Cost | Ability |
|-------|------|---------|
| **Banana Shooter** | 20 chips | Fires regular projectiles at monkeys |
| **Triple Banana** | 50 chips | Shoots three projectiles at once |
| **Frozen Banana** | 30 chips | Slows down monkeys it hits |
| **Rotten Banana** | 20 chips | Deals damage over time to monkeys |
| **Banana Peel** | 10 chips | One-time trap that eliminates the first monkey that steps on it |

**Monkey Types:**

- **Chimp**: Balanced speed and health
- **Lemur**: Fast but fragile
- **Gorilla**: Slow but very tanky

**Tips:**

- Click coins that spawn on the grid for extra resources
- Place towers strategically at choke points
- Mix tower types for maximum effectiveness
- Frozen Bananas work great with high-damage towers
- Banana Peels are cheap and perfect for early waves

**Controls:**

- Click a banana type to select it
- Click a grid cell to place the selected banana
- Drag and drop also works!
- Click coins to collect them
- Use the â† Home button to return to the main menu

### 3. ğŸ Banana Catcher

A fast-paced arcade game where you catch falling fruits while avoiding the bad ones!

**How to Play:**

- Move your basket to catch yellow bananas (+1 point)
- Avoid catching apples, oranges, and pears (lose a life)
- Missing a banana also costs a life
- Game gets progressively harder as you play

**Special Features:**

- **Extra Lives**: Catch 5 bananas in a row without mistakes to earn an extra life (max 5 lives)
- **Difficulty Scaling**: The game speeds up as you score higher
- **Persistent Difficulty**: Your difficulty level is saved between sessions

**Tips:**

- Focus on positioning - anticipate where fruits will fall
- Don't chase everything - sometimes it's safer to let bad fruits fall
- Build up consecutive banana catches for extra lives
- The difficulty indicator shows your current challenge level

**Controls:**

- Arrow Keys (Left/Right) â†’ Move basket
- A/D Keys â†’ Move basket (alternative)
- Click "Start" to begin
